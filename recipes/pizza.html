Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat, round bread covered with tomato sauce and cheese, typically with additional toppings such as sausage or pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  • Bread dough

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Spread tomato sauce on bread dough.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni or sausage.
  4. Bake in oven for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Enjoy your delicious pizza!
