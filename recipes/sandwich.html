Sandwich

A sandwich is a type of food that typically consists of two slices of bread with a filling in between them. The most common fillings for sandwiches are meats, cheeses, and vegetables, but there are many other possibilities.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Filling
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Choose your bread. There are many different types of bread available, so choose the one that you think will best complement your sandwich filling.
  2. Choose your filling. This can be anything from meats and cheeses to vegetables and spreads.
  3. Choose your condiments. These can include sauces, spreads, and dressings.
  4. Assemble your sandwich. Place the bread on a plate or cutting board, and add the filling and condiments.
  5. Enjoy!
