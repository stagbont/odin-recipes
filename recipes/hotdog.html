Hotdog

A hot dog is a grilled or steamed sausage sandwich where the sausage is served in a sliced bun.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Relish
  • Onions
  • cheese(optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat grill, broiler, or stovetop to cook hot dogs.
  2. Place hot dogs on bun.
  3. Add your favorite toppings: ketchup, mustard, relish, onions, and cheese.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
